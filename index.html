Salaried Documents

This applies even if there are multiple employments.

  • Recent 6 months’ payslips.
  • 1-year bank statement showing salary credits.
  • 3 years of employment proof required (if not available, an internal decision will be taken; please call us).
  • Company ID card.
  • 2 years PF statement (if there are PF deductions).
  • 2 years Form 16 (if there are deductions).

Self Employed Documents

Documented Income:

  • Income tax returns for the last 2 years.
  • GST for 1 year (if applicable).
  • Business proof.
  • Business vintage must be at least 3 years.
  • 1-year bank statements showing business transactions.
  • Purchase/sale bills (if available).

Undocumented Income:

  • Business proof (Udyam registration or any relevant business documents).
  • 1-year bank statement showing business transactions.
  • Handwritten records (if any, maintained by the customer).
  • Purchase bills / Sales records (if available).
